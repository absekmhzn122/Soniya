Soniya is here to remind every girl that she is strong, capable, and never alone.

Becoming Soniya

Hi! I’m Soniya Shrestha — a dreamer, creator, and go-getter. I believe in living life with purpose, kindness, and a little bit of sparkle. Whether I’m learning something new, helping others, or just enjoying the little things, I always try to stay true to myself. I’m passionate about fashion, travel, helping people, and I love turning ideas into something beautiful and meaningful. Life isn’t always perfect, but I believe every girl has the strength to rise, glow, and grow.
